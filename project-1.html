Project Overview

DietMasterGo+ is a professional meal plan system that includes recommended meal plans, healthy grocery lists, food and activity logging tools and messaging system that can only be activated by an authorized nutrition counselor. Login to DietMasterGo+ by using your username and password that is provided by the counselor when your web based cloud account is set up. Your nutritional profile is setup by the counselor on your web cloud account, then pushed to the DietMasterGo+ app. The nutritional plan and weight management goals include your daily calorie budget, goal weight, BMI, macronutrient ratios and other important factors that attribute to better overall health, nutritional habits and weight control.

How it works: Once you login to DietMasterGo+ app you can access your recommended meal plans and grocery lists. The food and activity logger is available to track the daily amount of calories consumed or burned during activities, and to compare those numbers with what has been established by your counselor. DietMasterGo+ cloud account also allows logging online via a web based portal. Regardless if you log on your phone or the cloud account, all data is sync'd up and down. This logged information can then be viewed and monitored by your nutrition counselor for better coaching and compliance to your personal plan.
